How It Works

Explore

Venture into the real world to discover Pokémon in your surroundings. Visit PokéStops and Gyms to collect items and battle.

Catch

Use Poké Balls to catch Pokémon you encounter. Different types of Poké Balls increase your chances of catching rare Pokémon.

Train Together

Join forces with other Trainers to take on powerful Raid Battles and earn special rewards.

Become Stronger

Power up your Pokémon, evolve them, and train them to become the ultimate Pokémon Master.

Special Events

Participate in exciting events, Community Days, and seasonal celebrations to catch rare Pokémon and earn exclusive rewards.

Global Community

Connect with Trainers worldwide, trade Pokémon, and compete in global challenges to unlock special features and rewards.
