Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start playing Pokémon GO?

Download the app from your device's app store, create an account, customize your trainer, and you're ready to start catching Pokémon in the real world!

Is Pokémon GO free to play?

Yes, Pokémon GO is free to download and play. Optional in-game purchases are available for items and upgrades.

How do I catch Pokémon?

When you encounter a Pokémon, tap on it to enter catch mode. Swipe Poké Balls toward the Pokémon to catch it. Timing and accuracy matter!

What are PokéStops?

PokéStops are real-world locations where you can collect items like Poké Balls, eggs, and other useful items. They refresh every few minutes.
