Frequently Asked Questions

How do I level up faster?

Use Lucky Eggs during evolution sprees, participate in raids, complete research tasks, and make excellent throws to maximize XP gain. Add friends and increase friendship levels for huge XP bonuses!

How do I get more Stardust?

Catch lots of Pokémon (especially weather-boosted ones), hatch eggs, complete research tasks, and participate in events. Star Pieces can boost your Stardust earnings by 50%.

What's the best way to win raids?

Build teams that counter the raid boss's type, power up your best counters, coordinate with other trainers, and use appropriate battle parties. Remote raid passes can help you join raids from anywhere!

How do I improve in PvP battles?

Learn type effectiveness, practice timing your shields and charge moves, build balanced teams, and understand the meta for each league. IV spreads matter differently in PvP than in raids.
