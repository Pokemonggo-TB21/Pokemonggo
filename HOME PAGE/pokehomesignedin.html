Features & Activities

Explore

Venture into the real world to discover Pokémon in your surroundings. Visit PokéStops and Gyms to collect items and battle.

Daily Tasks

Complete daily research tasks, catch your daily Pokémon, and earn special rewards. Track your progress and achievements.

Raid Battles

Join forces with other Trainers to take on powerful Raid Battles. Coordinate with your team and earn exclusive rewards.

Battle League

Compete in the GO Battle League, climb the ranks, and prove yourself as the ultimate Trainer in PvP battles.

Events

Participate in special events, Community Days, and seasonal celebrations to catch rare Pokémon and earn exclusive rewards.

Trading

Trade Pokémon with other Trainers, build your collection, and help complete each other's Pokédex entries.
